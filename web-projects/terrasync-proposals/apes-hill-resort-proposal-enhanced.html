Deployment Options

Flexible operational models designed to fit your course management style

Standard Stationary Model
Mowers positioned over specific zones with virtual mapping. Continuous autonomous coverage with local charging stations. Perfect for dedicated area maintenance with consistent, reliable operation.
Drop-and-Go Model
Battery swap technology enables central housing with team deployment to specific zones. More cost-effective and flexible, allowing dynamic coverage based on daily needs and conditions.
Hybrid Configuration
Best of both worlds - drop-and-go mowers with stationary capabilities. Full flexibility to adapt operations as your needs change, ensuring maximum return on investment.
