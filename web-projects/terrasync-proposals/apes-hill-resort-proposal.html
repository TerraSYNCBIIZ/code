Mowing Deployment Options

Standard Stationary Model
Stationary mowers are positioned over specific zones and defined areas on virtual maps. They continuously mow their designated areas and charge locally within their assigned zones. This model provides consistent, autonomous coverage for dedicated areas.
Drop-and-Go Model
Mowers with battery swap technology can be housed centrally and deployed by your team to specific zones as needed. When completed, mowers are collected and returned to the central housing. This efficient model is typically more cost-effective and offers maximum operational flexibility.
Hybrid Configuration
Drop-and-go mowers also have stationary capabilities with charging stations. If desired, any drop-and-go mower can be configured for stationary operation, providing ultimate flexibility to adapt to your operational preferences and requirements.
