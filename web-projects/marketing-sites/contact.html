Frequently Asked Questions

When should I book my holiday lighting installation?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks before your desired installation date. Our schedule fills up quickly during the holiday season!

Do you provide the lights or do I need to purchase them?

We provide all lighting equipment as part of our service. We use commercial-grade products designed to withstand winter weather.

What happens if lights stop working during the season?

We offer free service calls throughout the season to repair or replace any lights that stop working.

When do you take the lights down?

We typically schedule takedowns in January, but we can accommodate your specific preferences.
